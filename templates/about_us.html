About Us

Group BWK1A1

We’re motivated students at KU Leuven in Belgium studying engineering sciences. We received an assignment from ESAT (Electrical Engineering Department) to create a smart grid for efficient car charging. Initially, we were strangers and the assignment was vague, but over time, we got to know each other and became friends, which was crucial for our collaboration on this project. As the project progressed, the objectives became clearer, allowing us to develop a vision and workflow to successfully complete it.

Our motivation stemmed from the project's contribution to a greener future. Not only did we learn about sustainable charging solutions, but we also gained valuable experience working in a group environment—an essential skill we will utilize as engineers tackling larger, more complex challenges.
